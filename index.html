Mining Beauty Product Reviews

Sentiment, Demographics, and Patterns of Consumer Preference

Learn More

Overview

This project aims to integrate structured product data with unstructured customer reviews to uncover patterns. These patterns could be in consumer preferences, analyze the alignment between ratings and sentiments, and build models that explain or predict product success and customer satisfaction.

Methods

  • Method 1: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  • Method 2: Sentiment Analysis
  • Method 3: Association Rule Mining
